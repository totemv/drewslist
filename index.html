Drew's List

A hyper-local textbook marketplace created for Students by Students

What's Drew's List?

No more need to make flyers, search untrustworthy local listings, or crawl the web for books. Drew’s List is a new, simple way to sell and buy textbooks. Make extra cash by unloading past semester’s books and meet up with friends and other students on your campus.

Make Extra Cash

Sell all your obsolete textbooks to other peers on your campus. It's a win-win for all.

Find the Best Buyers

You don't need to wait around for buyers to come and find you. Drew's List automatically pairs you with the best buyer out there. You can start chatting as soon as you list your book.

Scan, two, three!

Let's keep it simple, shall we? Create your "Wishlist" or "Salelist" of books fast and easily with our automatic ISBN scanner.
